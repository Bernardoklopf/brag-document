# LinkedIn messages replies

Some examples of replies that I've sent to recruiters on LinkedIn.
I've used them as templates to give good reply to recruiters.

Some example of replies can be found in the `archived-content/recruiters-replies` folder.

## Positives replies

Thank you for introducing me to this opportunity!

I'd like to know more about this opportunity at {company}. Please tell me more about the project, seniority level, and requirements.

I'm gonna send you my PDF resume.

Thanks! Regards,
